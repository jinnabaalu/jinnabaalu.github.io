Who I Am

DevOps Architect and Site Reliability Engineer with 12+ years of experience in containerization, distributed systems, and cloud infrastructure. I’ve designed and managed high-availability distributed clusters, implemented observability-driven development, and optimized CI/CD pipelines across startups and enterprises.

Recognized as the ‘Value Ambassador for Wisestep Product’ for single-handedly designing, managing, and scaling Wisestep Inc.’s entire infrastructure—transforming it from the ground up into a fully optimized and self-sustaining system.

As a Platform Engineer and DevOps Manager, I have worked across all areas of application development—from scaffolding and containerized deployment to Observability-Driven Development, security, and performance optimization.

VibhuviOiO

In late 2024, I channeled over a decade of infrastructure experience into VibhuviOiO—an open-source infrastructure company that builds production-ready DevOps and platform engineering tools.

The rise of AI-powered development tools like Amazon Q, Claude, and GitHub Copilot gave me the confidence to move fast and build products solo, leveraging the deep domain knowledge I’d built over the years. What once required entire teams can now be prototyped and shipped by a single experienced engineer with the right tooling.

Professional Journey

DevOps Architect and Site Reliability Engineer with 12+ years of experience in containerization, distributed systems, and cloud infrastructure. I've designed and managed high-availability distributed clusters, implemented observability-driven development, and optimized CI/CD pipelines across startups and enterprises.

Recognized as the 'Value Ambassador for Wisestep Product' for single-handedly designing, managing, and scaling Wisestep Inc.'s entire infrastructure — transforming it from the ground up into a fully optimized and self-sustaining system.

As a Platform Engineer and DevOps Manager, I have worked across all areas of application development — from scaffolding and containerized deployment to Observability-Driven Development, security, and performance optimization.

In mid-2025, I channeled this experience into VibhuviOiO — an open-source infrastructure company that builds production-ready tools and provides consulting for teams of all sizes.

Personal Life

I'm a father and a responsible member of a joint family of 13. The first in my family to attend school, I went on to earn a Master's Degree in Computer Applications.

I'm a proud nerd with a busy life — balancing family, a full-time job, and my hobbies often feels like juggling ten balls at once. We all have the same 24 hours in a day, and time waits for no one.

To stay productive, I follow the GTD (Getting Things Done) methodology and have developed my own habits and disciplines over the years. My thoughts and projects live across different stages — some partially completed in GitHub, others stopped at the initial design stage, and many captured as stories in Google Keep, waiting to be revisited.

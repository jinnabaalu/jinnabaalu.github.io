Natural Farmer with the Support of My Brother

I am a natural farmer and heirloom seed conservator, working alongside my brother. My focus is on sustainable farming practices and preserving heirloom seeds to maintain their purity for future generations. Through natural farming, I contribute to climate change mitigation and promote biodiversity in agriculture.

News & Press

Eenadu — వరి సేద్యం.. వైవిధ్యం (Rice Farming Diversity)

Featured in Eenadu, one of India's largest Telugu-language newspapers. The article covers our natural farming journey — three brothers (Balu, Raju, Krishna) from Narsingi village, Papannapet, Medak cultivating 130 indigenous rice varieties across 17 acres using Subhash Palekar Natural Farming methods, with Gramabharati's collaboration. Highlights include heirloom seed conservation, Jeevaamrutam preparation, and building a sustainable agricultural model without chemical inputs.
